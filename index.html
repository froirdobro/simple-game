Welcome to my game

This is a very simple game! The only objective is constantly change the ball direction and avoid the obstacles with the pass of time!
The longer you survive, the higher score you get.
The game will over as soon as the ball hits one of the many obstacles
To control the direction of the ball just press Space bar
The ball also bounces when it hit the borders, this can be used as an advantage but it can also be your worst enemy!
